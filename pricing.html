Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?

Your 7-day free trial gives you full access to all LOOP features with no restrictions. No credit card required to start, and you can cancel anytime.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes! You can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation. You can export all your notes and continue using them elsewhere, or reactivate your account.

Is there a student discount?

Yes! Students receive 50% off Pro plans with a valid .edu email address. Contact us for verification and discount application.

How secure is my data?

We use enterprise-grade encryption and security measures. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and we never share your information with third parties.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, we'll provide a full refund, no questions asked.
